Personal Income Tax Declaration for Paying Salaries and Wages (02/KK-TNCN)

Report Layout

Descriptions of data generating

- Article [22]:

Counts number of personal that gets income which belongs to “A01”, “A02” Income Type and deducted PIT in the period.

- Article [23]:

Counts number of personal that gets income which belongs to “B01” Income Type and deducted PIT in the period.

- Article [25]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A01

- Article [26]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A02

- Article [27]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as B01

- Article [29]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A01 and “Tax Amount” > 0

- Article [30]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as A02 and “Tax Amount” > 0

- Article [31]:

Total “Taxable Income” of arising with “Income Type” as B01 and “Tax Amount” > 0

- Article [33]:

Total PIT arising in period

- Article [34]:

Total PIT of arising with “Income Type” as A02.

- Article [35]:

Total “Tax Amount” of arising with “Income Type” as B01
